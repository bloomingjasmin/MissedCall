About me

Hi, I am Jasmin Normatova! Through this piece I wanted to explore the weight of the past and how it lingers over us.
For me, that haunting feeling only begins to lift when we make a conscious choice to let it go.
I hope this work encourages you to unburden yourself too, to finally set the past free and create space for the present.
